Objective

At the objectives & principles working group it was decided to start a POC. The objective of the POC are as follows:
- So give members a feel of how to work in an open source environment.
- To start getting a idea of the different technologies that can be used for the actuarial library.

Instructions

The excel workbook has a valuation model for a simple life insurance contract. The objective is to replicate the model in your chosen technology.
Also provided are a mortality table, same policy data, a yield curve and lapse rates. These files are all the inputs that are required for the model.

Good luck and enjoy.
